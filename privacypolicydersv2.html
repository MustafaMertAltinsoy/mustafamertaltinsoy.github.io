Local storage (Hive)

All user data created by the App (notes, tasks, schedule entries, and any other App data) is stored locally on the user’s device using Hive. We do not back up or transmit this user data to our servers by default.

Retention — Data remains on the device until the user deletes the App or removes data via the App (if such an in-App delete feature exists). Uninstalling the App removes local data from the device according to the device's behavior and the Hive storage lifecycle.

4. Data Deletion

If you want your App data removed, uninstall the App from your device. Because data is stored locally (Hive), uninstalling the App is the primary way to remove stored data. If you need additional assistance, contact us at the email below and we will advise on available options; note that because data is stored only on the device, we cannot delete data from a device we do not have access to.
